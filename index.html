Spice & Sip

Spice & Sip by Sohan Patil is Pune’s vibrant culinary destination where bold spices meet fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. Offering a unique fusion of flavors from around the world, we specialize in mouthwatering grilled dishes, zesty flavors, and signature cocktails that promise to elevate your dining experience. Our mission is to create a welcoming space where friends, family, and food lovers can come together to savor unforgettable meals and drinks in a lively atmosphere.
